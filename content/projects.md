## Fuzzy Matching Engine for Banker Targeting (Citi)
**Problem**  
Bankers lacked accurate visibility into prospect relationships due to fragmented and inconsistent data.

**Solution**  
Built a **Python-based fuzzy matching engine** (94% accuracy) to process 200K+ records and map customer–product relationships.

**Impact**
- Enabled data-driven expansion strategies
- Integrated into Tableau dashboards for banker consumption
- Improved marketing targeting precision by **30%**

**Tools:** Python, FuzzyWuzzy, Pandas, Tableau
